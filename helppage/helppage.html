Help / FAQ

Do I need anything special to use TweetTweet?

All you need to use TweetTweet is an internet connection or a mobile phone. Join us! Once you're in, begin finding and following accounts whose Tweets interest you. We'll recommend great accounts once you're signed up.

What's a Tweet?

A Tweet is any message posted to TweetTweet which may contain photos, videos and text up to 140 characters. Once you've signed up, just type your first Tweet in the update box. Click the Tweet button to post the update to your profile.

How do I sign up / login / logout?

If you wish to sign up, please click here and follow the instructions provided on this page. If you wish to login, simply go to our login page and enter the necessary login credentials. If you wish to logout, find the "logout" button in the top right corner of your screen on the homepage.

I forgot my username / password. What do I do?

In the event that you forget your password, please navigate yourself to the login page, and click the "Forgot Username / Password?" link below where you are required to enter your login information.
