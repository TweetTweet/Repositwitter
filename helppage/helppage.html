About Us

TweetTweet is a service for friends, family anyone. The purpose is to communicate and stay connected through the exchange of quick, frequent messages. People post Tweets, which may contain photos, videos, links and up to 140 characters of text

Help / FAQ

Do I need anything special to use TweetTweet?

All you need to use TweetTweet is an internet connection. Join us today!

What's a Tweet?

A Tweet is any message posted to TweetTweet which may contain photos, videos and text.

How do I sign up / login / logout?

If you wish to sign up, please navigate to our login page, click on "Register" and follow the instructions provided on this page. If you wish to login, simply go to our login page and enter your login credentials. If you wish to logout, locate the "logout" button in the bottom left corner of your screen on the homepage.
